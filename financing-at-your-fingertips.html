FINANCING AT YOUR FINGERTIPS

June 28th, 2017

Although the prospect of finances may sound intimidating, today’s technology has made handling finances easy and accessible to students through smartphones. Read on to learn how to use a variety of convenient apps to easily manage your money with your smartphone.

Online Banking Apps

Both the Big 5 Banks and emerging retail banks have created online banking apps (learn more about the different banks here). With these apps you can easily bank anywhere, anytime, without ever having to step into a branch. Transactions such as transferring money, depositing a cheque, writing a loan application, or even making investments can be done wherever you are.

With an online banking app, you have the added convenience of being able to track your account activity, which helps keep your account more secure and allows you to keep track of your spending.

Budgeting Apps

If you’ve ever found yourself overspending on food, coffee, or clothes (I know I have), budgeting apps are for you. They make creating and managing budgets easy and hassle-free. Mint, for example, syncs to your bank account and spending activity, and creates personalized budgets for you, alerting you when you begin to overspend.

Mobile Payments or Digital Wallets

If you’re always forgetting your wallet, or your greatest fear is losing it, you should consider using a digital wallet. Digital wallets allow you to make purchases with your credit card or debit card from your phone, which, as Gen Z-ers, we always have with us. Digital wallets can also hold boarding passes, loyalty cards, and gift cards.

Be sure to download some of these apps! The small device in your pocket, can help you easily access the world of finance. Take advantage of its quick convenience, be smart with your money, and create #MoreMoneyMoreMoments.
