Learn to Make Money While You Spend

by Rayah Ladak

Learn to Make Money While You Spend

June 28th, 2017

As the summer is quickly approaching, it’s time to start updating your wardrobe, travelling, and trying new restaurants. But with all this adventure comes a cost. So how can we earn by spending money? Loyalty programs are the answer. Loyalty programs for different stores can earn you rewards or discounts on your purchases.

The SPC Card

The Student Price Card (SPC Card) is one of the easiest ways to save money when shopping or eating out. This card is initially $10, but the savings you can get make up for the charge. Offers can vary depending on the store, but most are 10%-15% off your whole purchase. Although, if you bank BMO you can get the No Fee BMO SPC, which means you can reap the rewards without the service fee of $10. Here is where you can see all the deals available for students.

Summerlicious/Winterlicious

Summerlicious and Winterlicious are amazing deals on food in Toronto. For two weeks in the summer and winter, you can enjoy mouth-watering food from the top restaurants around Toronto. The restaurants will have a fixed three course menu for a fixed, affordable price. The prices for lunch will only be $18-$28 and the dinners are $28-$48. The amazing restaurants that you can make reservations at are Canoe, Momofuku, and Colette’s. During these weeks you can dine like royalty, without having to be as rich as royalty.

Scene Points

Cineplex Scene points are another great way to earn free items. For every movie you see, you can earn 100 points for general admission and 200 for VIP cinemas. After accumulating 1000 points, you earn a free movie. You can earn free Scene Points when playing TimePlay in theatres. Your Scene points are an easy way to get free movies, and further enjoy your experiences.

Starbucks Rewards

Finally, the one I probably use the most: The Starbucks Rewards program . Since most of Gen Z are Starbucks customers anyway, it’s important that we earn back some of the money we spend. For every $1 you spend, you earn two stars. You are also able to earn bonus stars through personalized offers. For every 125 stars you earn, you can get a free item at Starbucks, whether it be food or drinks of any size. Along with free items when you earn 125 stars, you can get a free drink on your birthday!

It’s always important to sign up for loyalty systems, especially when you are a frequent customer. By joining loyalty programs, you are able to earn back money that you spend! Besides, things are just better when they’re free.
